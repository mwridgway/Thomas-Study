Where to Find Official Past Papers

Past papers are published by the Department of Basic Education and provincial education departments. Access them directly from these official sources:

  • Department of Basic Education (National) — Official NSC exam papers and memos for all subjects
  • Provincial Education Departments — Provincial preparatory exam papers
  • Your school — Internal exam papers and trial exams

Papers are typically available as PDF downloads. Always practise under timed conditions for the best preparation.

How to Use Past Papers Effectively

  • Time yourself — Simulate real exam conditions (3 hours for most papers)
  • Mark your own work — Use the memo to identify gaps in your knowledge
  • Track your scores — Aim to improve with each attempt
  • Identify patterns — Notice which topics appear frequently
  • Work backwards — Start with the most recent year and work backwards
  • Focus on weak areas — Spend extra time on questions you get wrong
